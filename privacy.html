LumaFlow Gii – Privacy Policy

Desktop photo uploader for Google Drive and Google Photos.

1. Overview

LumaFlow Gii is a desktop application that helps photographers upload images from their computer or camera to their own Google Drive and Google Photos accounts.

2. What data we access

The app can access the following data, only after you sign in with Google and grant permission:

  • Files you choose to upload to your Google Drive (scope: drive.file).
  • Your Google Photos library, only to add new photos and albums (scope: photoslibrary.appendonly).
  • Optionally, read-only access to photos in your Google Photos / Drive if you enable preview features (scope: drive.photos.readonly).

3. How data is used

  • Photos are uploaded only to your own Google account (Drive / Photos) that you sign in with.
  • Access tokens from Google are stored locally on your device to keep you signed in.
  • We do not use your data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

4. Local storage on your computer

LumaFlow Gii stores configuration files and OAuth tokens locally on your computer (for example, in your user profile directory). These files are used only by the app to authenticate and remember your settings.

5. Third-party services

The app uses Google APIs to upload and manage your photos and files. Your use of Google services is also governed by Google’s Privacy Policy and Google’s Terms of Service.

6. Your choices

  • You can revoke the app’s access at any time from your Google Account security settings.
  • You can remove local configuration and token files from your computer to fully sign out.
